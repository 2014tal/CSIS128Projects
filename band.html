Disturbed

Band members

David Draiman-vocals
Dan Donegan-lead guitar
Mike Wengren-Drums
John Moyer-Bass

Albums

  • The Sickness - The bands most successful album and the first one they ever released. I'd reccomend it to anyone who likes heavy metal and hard rock.
  • Believe - The band second album and a bit of a departure from their usual style. The album includes more melodic pieces that complment the hard rock and overall is an excellent album.
  • Immortalized - The bands newest album. Released to mixed reception, this is the band leased well preforming album to date, but includes a few good songs in their normal style.
